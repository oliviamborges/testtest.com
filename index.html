Framework

  • Competence

    While other funding opportunities are available to any Harvard student, only Test and Propel members are eligible for Spark Grants. Awards of up to $2,000 help members complete demand-validation experiments for their product or service.

  • Emotional Stability

    Select ventures in our student membership have access to our popular mentor program. Founders are matched with a mentor who will meet regularly to support your venture and help make progress on your unique goals and needs.

  • Engagement

    Members receive discounts on venture-building resources, like Wix and AWS. Log in to the My i-lab portal to access your discount codes.

  • Meaning

    Ventures in the Test and Propel stages are considered for promotional opportunities, including venture profiles, feature stories, social media posts, and more. Participants in our President's Innovation Challenge enjoy additional promotion.

  • Optismism

    While many of our events and workshops are open to all Harvard students, members enjoy exclusive access events such as our orientation, cohort meetups, member socials, and the Gong-a-thon.
